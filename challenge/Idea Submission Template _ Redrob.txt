Team Name : Sarva Automata
Problem Statement : Rank 100K candidates for a Senior AI Engineer (search/retrieval/ranking) role with NDCG@10 as primary metric, CPU-only runtime, and honeypot-aware quality.
Team Leader Name : Arjun Mangarath

Solution Overview
What is your proposed solution?
Hybrid recall (BM25 + BGE dense + career RRF) → LightGBM LambdaRank on 45+ features → BGE cross-encoder rerank on top-700, with Optuna-tuned CE/LTR/RRF fusion. Post-fusion modifiers enforce availability in top-30, limit template clones, and down-rank research-only profiles.

What differentiates your approach from traditional candidate matching systems?
We combine IR-style retrieval with learned ranking and Redrob behavioral signals as first-class multipliers—not keyword stuffing. Trap/honeypot rules and availability caps address adversarial profiles explicitly called out in the challenge README.

JD Understanding & Candidate Evaluation
What are the key requirements extracted from the JD?
5–8 YOE production ML; search/retrieval/ranking systems; embeddings + vector search; India hubs (Pune/Noida/Bangalore); product engineering over pure research; recruiter reachability (response rate, notice).

Which candidate signals are most important for determining relevance? / How does your solution evaluate candidate fit beyond keyword matching?
Silver-tier labels + LTR features cover skills, career evidence, GitHub activity, and trap flags. Behavioral signals (response rate, recency, notice) break ties among template-clone summaries. Cross-encoder scores semantic fit to JD without runtime LLM calls.

Ranking Methodology
How does your system retrieve, score, and rank candidates?
BM25 and dense BGE retrieve ~2K; LTR scores with trap/behavior penalties; top-700 fused with cross-encoder; availability cap in top-30; export top-100 with fused-derived monotonic scores.

What models, algorithms, or heuristics are used?
BM25, FAISS, LightGBM LambdaRank, BGE-reranker-base, honeypot/trap heuristics, Optuna modifier tuning on silver proxy NDCG.

How are multiple candidate signals combined into a final ranking?
Weighted fusion (CE/LTR/RRF) × behavioral multiplier × post-fusion penalties for low availability, summary clones, and research titles without production evidence.

Explainability & Data Validation
How are ranking decisions explained?
Five rank-band reasoning templates cite YOE, skills, location, availability, production vs research, and eval metrics (NDCG/MAP) from whitelisted fields only.

How do you prevent hallucinations or unsupported justifications?
No LLM at rank time; reasoning built from structured profile/signals with explicit gap flags (notice, GitHub, availability).

How does your solution handle inconsistent, low-quality, or suspicious profiles?
Hard-exclude honeypots; subtractive trap penalties; steep availability down-rank; template-clone detection; research-title penalty without production keywords.

End-to-End Workflow
What is the complete workflow from JD input to ranked candidate output?
Preprocess: features + embeddings + silver labels + LTR train (offline, ~90 min). Rank: load artifacts → hybrid recall → LTR → CE rerank → fusion + modifiers → CSV + audit.

System Architecture
Offline: preprocess.py, train_ltr.py, tune_modifiers.py. Online: rank.py / FastAPI HF sandbox. Artifacts: parquet features, FAISS, BM25, ltr_model.lgb, fusion_params.json, modifier_params.json.

Results & Performance
What results or insights demonstrate ranking quality?
Proxy NDCG@10 1.0; NDCG@30 0.9922; tier-5 in top 10/30/100 = 9/26/70; 0 honeypots in top-100; 0 low-availability in top-30 after cap.

How does your solution meet the challenge's runtime and compute constraints?
Precomputed embeddings/index (~90 min offline); rank.py ~5–8 min CPU, 16GB RAM, no network at inference.

Technologies Used
What technologies, frameworks, and tools were used and why were they selected for this solution?
Python, Polars, LightGBM, sentence-transformers, FAISS, rank-bm25, FastAPI, Optuna, Gemini (offline JD/labels only), Cursor for engineering.
